Who we are

Together, we're building a brighter future.

The Ologodidan Jezi Foundation is established to further advance the Humanitarian Services towards the Vulnerable in our Society, embarked upon over the years in FCT and across the South -West and North Central States of Nigeria.

Evangelist Rachael Ologodidan - Founder

At Ologodidan Jevi Foundation, our story is one of dedication and empathy. Founded with a vision to uplift communities and create lasting change, we're driven by the belief that every individual deserves support and opportunities for a better tomorrow.
