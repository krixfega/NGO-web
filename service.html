The Foundation

Called forth to transform lives

The Ologodidan Jezi Foundation is established to further advance the Humanitarian Services towards the Vulnerable in our Society, embarked upon by Evangelist Rachael Ologodidan over the years in FCT and across the South -West and North Central States of Nigeria. President of Ologodidan Multi -Purpose Cooperative Society Limited in Kwara State, Abuja and Lagos State. Through Hard -work, Diligent and Heart of Kindness, the Cooperative Society grew into a Group of Companies like Ologodidan Multiservice Limited which she's also the MD/CEO.

Evangelist Rachael is a Woman full of compassion. Many Lives of less privileged persons have been touched and changed by her. To God be the Glory that an Orphan like her is today feeding and clothing many Orphans. Widows, Elderly and Physically Challenged Persons are not left out in her empowerment programmes. She has visited Kirikiri Correctional Centre in Lagos and Kuje Correctional Centre in Abuja with food for the Inmates. She is a Giver! She is a Bridge -Builder! She is a Beacon of Hope to the future generation! Evangelist Racheal has empowered the poor with Buses, Tricycles (Keke Napep), Household items like Deep Freezers, Plasma Televisions, etc. to make life better for them. Two years ago, over 200 Persons in Ikotun, Lagos State were fed through her Foundation In addition, Widows have received money to start micro businesses to help their Families. Through her benevolence, troubled marriages and homes have been re-united

Welcome to Ologodidan Jevi Foundation, a Non Governmental Organization where every act of kindness creates ripples of change. Join us as we build a brighter and more equitable world together.
